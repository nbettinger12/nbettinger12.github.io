Mission Statement:

True Pastafarians know that pirates are Divine Beings and the original believers in our Lord, the Flying Spaghetti Monster. Sadly, the centuries of decline in swashbucklers has led to an increase of temperatures worldwide.

We at SuperPirates are here to serve up knowledge and solutions to this worldwide disaster. We are your stewards to the revival of the noble pirate. With our tireless efforts we can spread the message of the pirate and work to decrease global warming...and SAVE the WORLD!
